Intro to Fractions

What are Fractions?

In terms of mathematics and where it stands in the level of math and science, fractions are one step above normal mathematics and PMDAS (Parentheses, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction). The scientific idea of fractions is that it is a partition of a whole number. To bring the idea to real-world objects, fractions represent splitting them into “fractions.”

Let's say a student is given a long ceramic board and needs to split it into equal parts for both him and his partner for a project.

We can easily say, he can split the board in two so both he and his partner have a slab of ceramic board.

Now then, what if we want to split the board so that both members of the team have enough to do multiple attempts with them? Well, we can split the board some more. For now, let's split the board so both members have 3 boards to work with.
