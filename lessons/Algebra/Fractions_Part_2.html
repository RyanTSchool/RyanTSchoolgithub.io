Improper Fractions

When it comes to fractions, most of the time you will come across fractions large and small. Sometimes, you would even simplify the larger fractions into smaller fractions. However, some questions you run into will have fractions where the numerator is greater than or equal to the denominator. These fractions are called improper fractions.

What do Improper Fractions look like?

Here are a few examples of some fractions: 9⁄5, 11⁄3, 3⁄3
